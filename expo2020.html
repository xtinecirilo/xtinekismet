Expo2020

Logo designed for contest


EXPO 2020 - "Connecting Minds, Creating the Future" With this theme, I made a logo using polygons to make it look like a brain and used color blue as it is often associated with intelligence; thus make it look professional. Connectivity is demonstrated by color which creates overlap and transparency. Transparent connection shows how the components(polygons) work together for the future."X" is differ from others, as it is on top and symbolizes collaboration: two intersecting rounded polygons where the intersection represents the idea that will make it on top and achieve the goals.
